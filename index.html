Welcome, Doug.

You arrived at Oklahoma State University in 1982 as a freshman with a business degree ahead of you. You stayed through 1988, picking up a master's in business education along the way — and graduating the same year Barry Sanders won the Heisman Trophy.

In between, you watched the Cowboys play in the House of Horrors, walked The Strip on game nights, and lived through some of the most memorable moments of the 1980s. This site is a time capsule of all of it — the teams, the town, the times.
